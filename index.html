About TerraRiff
TerraRiff
TerraRiff is a rhythm game mod for Terraria.

It adds a fully playable 4-lane rhythm game inside Terraria.
  • 3 built-in songs
  • Customizable controls
  • Chart, share and play your own custom songs
TerraRiff is actively developed and expanding.
